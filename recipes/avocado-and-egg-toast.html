Avocado and Egg Toast

Description

A quick and easy breakfast supreme full of flavor. A household favorite!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices wheat bread
  • 4 tablespoons barbeque sauce
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Toast bread in a toaster until medium brown, about 2 minutes. Spread each slice equally with barbeque sauce.

  2. Melt butter in a small skillet over medium-high heat. Crack eggs in and fry on both sides, about 4 minutes.

  3. Place each fried egg on a piece of toast. Top each with 1/4 of an avocado. Sprinkle an equal amount of cheese on top of each toast.
